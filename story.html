Main Story

Joe was a child who lost his family at a young age, and at this age, his life in crime began with his street life, he became a very famous thief. In this process, Joe went to prison many times and learned that his best friend betrayed him when he last entered, and that Joe fled abroad with his wife after this news. He got very angry and found a way to escape from the prison and broke through the walls and now Joe has a revenge to take and the police to escape.
